# DEX Card Setup - Orderly One

> Configure how your DEX appears on the leaderboard

## Overview

This page allows you to customize your DEX's card appearance on the public leaderboard. Your DEX card is how other users will discover and learn about your platform.

## Card Components

### Visual Elements

1. **Banner Image**
   - Large header image for your DEX card
   - Recommended size: 1200x400px
   - Formats: PNG, JPG, WebP
   - Use eye-catching visuals that represent your brand

2. **Logo**
   - Square logo displayed on the card
   - Recommended size: 200x200px
   - Appears alongside your DEX name

### Information

3. **Description**
   - Brief description of your DEX
   - Max length: 500 characters
   - Explain what makes your DEX unique
   - Highlight key features or community focus

4. **Token Information** (Optional)
   - **Token Address**: Smart contract address
   - **Chain**: Which blockchain the token is on
   - **Symbol**: Token ticker (e.g., "USDC", "ETH")
   - **Name**: Full token name
   - Automatically fetches price and market cap data

### Social Links

5. **Community Links**
   - Telegram group
   - Discord server
   - Additional links specific to your DEX

## Token Integration

If your DEX has an associated token:
- Enter the token contract address
- Select the blockchain
- System automatically fetches:
  - Current price
  - Market capitalization
  - Token icon
  - Real-time data

## Best Practices

- **Banner**: Use high-quality images that represent your brand identity
- **Description**: Be clear and concise about your DEX's value proposition
- **Logo**: Use your primary brand logo for consistency
- **Token**: Only add if you have an official project token
- **Links**: Ensure all social links are active and moderated

## Visibility

**Important**: Your DEX card only appears on the public leaderboard after graduation. Set up your card early so it's ready when you graduate.

## Updates

You can update your DEX card at any time:
- Changes are reflected immediately
- No redeployment needed
- Update as your brand evolves
