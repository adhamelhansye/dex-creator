# Graduate Your DEX - Orderly One

> Enable revenue generation and start earning fees

## Overview

This page handles the graduation process - registering your DEX as an official Orderly Network broker to enable revenue generation from trading activity.

## What is Graduation?

Graduation is the process of:
1. Registering your DEX as an official broker with Orderly Network
2. Setting up fee configurations
3. Enabling revenue generation from trading activity
4. Unlocking additional features like referral programs

## Requirements

Before you can graduate:
- ✅ Have a custom domain configured
- ✅ DEX must be deployed
- ✅ Complete basic configuration

## Fee Configuration

### Broker Fee
- **What it is**: Your share of trading fees
- **Range**: Configurable basis points (bps)
- **Revenue**: Earned from all trading activity on your DEX

### Base Fee
- **What it is**: Orderly Network's infrastructure fee
- **Purpose**: Covers network costs and services
- **Standard**: Set by Orderly Network

### Calculator
The graduation page includes a fee calculator that shows:
- Total fees per trade
- Your revenue per trade
- Expected earnings based on volume

## Graduation Process

1. **Configure Fees**: Set your broker fee percentage
2. **Submit Transaction**: Pay the one-time graduation fee on-chain
3. **Verification**: System verifies your transaction
4. **Activation**: Your DEX starts earning fees from trading activity

## Post-Graduation

After graduating, you gain access to:
- **Fee Revenue**: Earn from all trading activity
- **Referral Program**: Set up auto-referral codes
- **Leaderboard**: Your DEX appears on the public leaderboard
- **Advanced Features**: Additional broker management tools

## Important Notes

- Graduation requires a one-time on-chain transaction
- Make sure you have the necessary tokens for the transaction
- The process is irreversible - ensure your configuration is correct
- Custom domain is required for graduation
