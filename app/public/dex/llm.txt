# Create Your DEX - Orderly One

> Manage and deploy your perpetual decentralized exchange

## Overview

This is the main DEX management dashboard where users can create, configure, and deploy their own perpetual decentralized exchange powered by Orderly Network.

## What You Can Do Here

1. **Create a New DEX**: Start building your perpetual trading platform
2. **View Deployment Status**: Monitor your DEX repository creation and GitHub Pages deployment
3. **Access Configuration**: Customize branding, themes, wallets, and features
4. **Graduate to Mainnet**: Move from testnet to production and start earning fees
5. **Setup DEX Card**: Configure how your DEX appears on the leaderboard
6. **Manage Custom Domain**: Add your own domain to your deployed DEX
7. **Access Referral Program**: Set up auto-referral codes (for graduated DEXs)

## Key Features

- **No-Code Deployment**: Automatically creates a GitHub repository and deploys to GitHub Pages
- **Configuration Management**: Full control over DEX settings and appearance
- **Fee Revenue**: Earn fee splits after graduating your DEX
- **Custom Domains**: Use your own domain for your DEX

## DEX Lifecycle

1. **Setup**: Create your DEX and configure initial settings
2. **Deployment**: Deploy your DEX
3. **Configuration**: Customize branding, features, and settings
4. **Custom Domain** (Optional): Add your own domain
5. **Graduation**: Register as a broker to enable revenue generation
6. **Earning**: Start earning fee revenue from trading activity

## Next Steps

- New users: Click "Create DEX" to get started
- Existing users: Configure your DEX settings or graduate to enable revenue generation
- Need help? Check the DEX Setup Assistant for guidance
