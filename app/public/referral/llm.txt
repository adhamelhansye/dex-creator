# Referral Program - Orderly One

> Manage your DEX referral program and earn from trader activity

## Overview

The referral program allows graduated DEX owners to set up auto-referral codes, incentivizing traders and growing their community while earning additional revenue.

## Requirements

- ✅ DEX must be graduated to mainnet
- ✅ Valid Orderly trading key
- ✅ Active broker ID

## How It Works

### Auto-Referral System

1. **Create Orderly Key**: Generate a trading key for your account
2. **Configure Referral**: Set up your auto-referral settings
3. **Automatic Application**: All traders using your DEX get your referral code
4. **Earn Rewards**: You earn from the trading activity of referred users

### Orderly Trading Key

The Orderly key is required to:
- Interact with Orderly Network's referral system
- Manage your referral settings
- Track referral performance

**Creating a Key**:
- One-click generation
- Securely stored in your browser
- Can be regenerated if needed

## Configuration

- **Referral Code**: Your unique code (auto-generated)

## Revenue Streams

As a graduated DEX owner, you earn from:
1. **Trading Fees**: Your broker fee from all trades
2. **Referral Rewards**: Additional earnings from referee activity
3. **Combined**: Maximizes revenue from your platform
