# DEX Leaderboard - Orderly One

> Discover top performing DEXs built on Orderly Network

## Overview

The leaderboard showcases all graduated DEXs built with Orderly One, ranked by trading performance and activity.

## Metrics Tracked

### Trading Volume
- **24 Hours**: Recent trading activity
- **7 Days**: Weekly performance
- **30 Days**: Monthly trends
- **All Time**: Cumulative trading volume

### PnL (Profit and Loss)
- Total trader profits/losses across the platform
- Indicator of trading activity and user engagement

### Broker Fees
- Revenue earned by the DEX owner
- Based on trading volume and fee configuration

### Total Fees
- Combined fees including Orderly Network fees
- Shows overall economic activity

## Leaderboard Features

### Sorting
- Sort by volume, PnL, fees
- Different time periods (24h, 7d, 30d, all-time)
- Discover trending and established DEXs

### DEX Cards
Each DEX is displayed with:
- Name and logo
- Description
- Banner image
- Key metrics
- Associated token (if any)
- Social links

### Filtering
- Search by DEX name
- Filter by time period
- Sort by different metrics

## Token Integration

DEXs with associated tokens show:
- Token price
- Market capitalization
- Token symbol and name
- Live price data

## For DEX Creators

Your DEX will appear on this leaderboard after:
1. Graduating your DEX (enabling revenue generation)
2. Generating trading activity
3. Setting up your DEX card (optional but recommended)

The leaderboard helps:
- Showcase your DEX to potential users
- Attract liquidity providers
- Build community awareness
- Track your performance vs other DEXs

## Use Cases

- **Traders**: Find active DEXs with good liquidity
- **Projects**: Discover potential integration partners
- **Creators**: Monitor competition and industry trends
- **Community**: See the growing Orderly Network ecosystem
